Privacy

Privacy Policy

Overview

MegaCinema is a client-side video player. We were built from the ground up to process as little personal data as possible. By using MegaCinema, you accept the practices described here.

What We Do NOT Collect

No Personal Information

We never ask for your name, email, or account details to use the player. No registration required.

No Video URL Logging

The URLs you paste are never sent to our servers. They stay entirely within your browser session.

No Location or Device Tracking

We do not collect GPS data, IP-based profiles, or hardware identifiers for personal profiling.

Local Storage (Browser Only)

The "Video History" and "Favorites" features save data to your browser's Local Storage — stored only on your device, never transmitted to us. You can clear it anytime using the "Clear History" button or your browser's cache settings.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g., mobile vs desktop usage). This data is anonymized — we cannot identify individual users from it. You can opt out via browser ad-blocking extensions or by disabling cookies.

Third-Party Video Hosts

When you load a video URL, your browser connects directly to that external server. That server may log your IP address. MegaCinema has no control over third-party privacy practices.

Advertising

We may display ads to support hosting costs. Ad networks may use their own cookies. We do not sell user data to advertisers — we simply don't have any to sell.

Security

All traffic through MegaCinema is protected by HTTPS (TLS encryption), guarding against interception on open networks.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

MegaCinema is a general-purpose tool. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information, contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as technology evolves. Changes are published on this page. Continued use of MegaCinema after updates implies acceptance.
