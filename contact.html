How to Classify Your Inquiry

Adding a category to your subject line helps us respond faster. Here are the main types:

01

Technical Support & Bug Reports

Use subject: [Bug Report]. Please include your OS, browser version, the video URL (if safe to share), and steps to reproduce the issue.

  • Operating system (e.g., Windows 11, iOS 17)
  • Browser and version (e.g., Chrome 124)
  • Steps taken before the bug appeared
  • Screenshots if possible
02

Feature Requests & Suggestions

Use subject: [Feature Request] Your idea. Describe how the feature would work and what problem it solves. Every suggestion is reviewed during our roadmap meetings.

03

Advertising & Partnerships

Use subject: [Partnership] Brand Name. Include your campaign goals, desired placement, duration, and budget range.

04

DMCA & Copyright Inquiries

MegaCinema does not host any video files — we are a client-side player only. Legally binding takedown notices must go to the actual file host (e.g., AWS, Google Drive). Use subject: [DMCA Inquiry] for guidance.

Expected Response Time

We typically reply within 24–48 business hours. During major updates or high-volume periods it may take slightly longer — but your message will never go unread.

Thank you for using MegaCinema. Your feedback is the foundation of everything we build.
