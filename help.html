Help Center

Quick answers to the most common questions about MegaCinema.

How do I play a video?

Paste a direct video URL into the input box on the home page and click Play Video. The player will load and start automatically.

How do I jump to a specific time?

Use the Go To Time field and enter a time like 30:00, 40:00, or total seconds like 1800. You can also use the −30s and +30s quick skip buttons.

Which formats are supported?

MegaCinema supports MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WEBM, FLV, M3U8 (HLS), and MPD (DASH). Playback depends on your browser's codec support and the server's CORS settings.

Why is my video not playing?

Common causes: invalid or expired URL, cross-origin (CORS) restrictions on the server, unsupported codec, or browser limitations. Try a different browser or test with a known-good MP4 link.

How do mobile gestures work?

Double-tap the left side to rewind 10s, right side to fast-forward 10s. Swipe up/down on the left for brightness, right side for volume.

How do I save a favorite?

Paste a URL, then click the ★ star icon in the Quick Controls section. Favorites are saved in your browser and persist across sessions.
